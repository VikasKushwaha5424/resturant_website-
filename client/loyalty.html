🏆 GreenBite Rewards

Eat more, earn more! Our loyalty program rewards you every time you order. Collect points and redeem them for free meals, discounts, and special perks.

How It Works

Simple, no app needed — just your phone number

📝

1. Sign Up Free

Just share your name and phone number at the counter or while ordering online. That's it, you're enrolled!

🪙

2. Earn Points

Get 1 point for every ₹10 spent. Points are automatically added to your account after every order.

🎁

3. Redeem Rewards

Use your points for free food, discounts, or special perks. Check the reward tiers below!

🔄

4. Keep Earning

Points never expire as long as you order at least once a month. Keep eating, keep earning!

Our Story

GreenBite was started by Vikas Kushwaha, a North Indian food lover who moved to Vizag for his studies at GITAM University. Missing the authentic flavors of home, he decided to bring the taste of North India to Vizag — but with a twist.

Vikas realized that students and locals alike craved both the rich curries of the North and the crispy dosas of the South. So GreenBite was born — a place where you can enjoy the best of both worlds under one roof.

Today, GreenBite is a favorite spot near GITAM campus, known for its generous thalis, flavorful curries, and that perfect cup of filter coffee after every meal.

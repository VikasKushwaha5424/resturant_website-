Privacy Policy & Terms

Home / Privacy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to GreenBite ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of our customers, especially students. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information through our website forms:

  • Name – When you make a reservation, contact us, or place an order.
  • Email address – For sending confirmations and responding to inquiries.
  • Phone number – To contact you about reservations, orders, or catering.
  • Delivery address – To deliver your food orders.
  • Order details – Items you add to cart are stored in your browser's local storage.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and deliver your food orders.
  • Confirm reservations and respond to inquiries.
  • Improve our services and menu based on feedback.
  • Contact you about catering requests or job applications.

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage

Your cart data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage and is not transmitted to any server. Form submissions are processed locally for demonstration purposes. In a production environment, data would be transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption.

5. Cookies

This website uses localStorage to remember your cart items across pages. We do not use tracking cookies or any third-party analytics tools.

6. Terms of Service

6.1 Orders & Payments

  • All prices are in Indian Rupees (₹) and include GST where applicable.
  • Menu items and prices may change without prior notice.
  • Orders once confirmed cannot be cancelled after preparation has started.
  • We accept UPI, Cash on Delivery, and Card payments.

6.2 Delivery

  • Delivery is available within a 5km radius of our restaurant.
  • Delivery fees may apply for orders under ₹500.
  • We are not responsible for delays caused by traffic or weather conditions.

6.3 Refunds

  • If you receive a wrong order, contact us within 30 minutes for a replacement or refund.
  • Refunds for meal plan cancellations are prorated, minus a ₹100 processing fee.

6.4 Catering

  • Catering bookings require at least 3 days advance notice.
  • A 50% advance payment is required for catering orders above ₹5,000.
  • Cancellation within 24 hours of the event may not be eligible for a refund.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
